--- layout: recipe title: Pastasalat med kremet gurkemeiesaus ---

Ingredients

    Pastasalat

  • 150g Pasta
  • 1.5ts Pepper
  • 125g Cherrytomater
  • 62.5g Salatmiks

    • Gurkemeiesaus

  • 1.25dl Fløte
  • 0.5ts Gurkemeie
  • 0.5stk Sitron skall revet
  • 0.25ts Chilli flak

Instructions

  1. Grill tomater i stekovn
  2. Kok pasta
  3. Bland alle ingrediensene i gurkemeiesausen med stavmikser
  4. Rør sausen inn i pastaen
  5. Bland alle ingrediensene
